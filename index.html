Use Cases

Suppose you want to try learning music production. You can set goals for yourself such as reading a book on music theory, taking an online course on using digital audio work station software, then creating a song with virtual instruments, and finally learning to mix in post production, etc. But youre busy, so you can only commit around three hours a week to it and you think you can accomplish all of those goals in three months. So hobby phase could schedule three 1-hour sessions for you to learn music production throughout the week, perhaps after work.
